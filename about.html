A Little Something About Me

by Ryan Helgerson

I taught English and Spanish to high school students for ten years. I worked with young people from New York, Texas, Abu Dhabi, and Las Vegas. They taught me a lot.

I took a sabbatical to focus on my family after my wife and I had children. I decided to work nights and weekends in the service industry so that we could avoid getting childcare before pre-school.

Searching for a new endeavor during this transition, I dove into a coding bootcamp at the University of Minnesota. I was enticed by the possibility of building content online rather than just consuming it.

The pandemic closed the restaurant where I worked, cancelled our weekly appointments, and sent us all indoors.

Since I was being told to spend most of my time inside, I reasoned that I might as well do it on the console. I enrolled in the Vanilla JavaScript Academy.

Now I split my time between absorbing tutorials and using what I learn to build things. Take a look around and reach out to me if I can build something for you or your organization.
